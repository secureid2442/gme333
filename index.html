Investors have been speculating on the turnaround of GameStop for quite some time.

GameStop announced a multi-year transformation initiative, which they refer to as GameStop Reboot, to position GameStop on a strategic path to fully leverage a unique position and brand in gaming. Redditors revitalized interest in GameStop after hedge funds were set on its bankruptcy. What happened next was the rise of GameStop’s stock GME, +9169.93%.

GameStop's strategic plan has been redesigned to stabilize and optimize their core business while at the same time pursuing strategic initiatives to transform the company for the future by expanding addressable market and product offerings to drive growth in the gaming and entertainment industries.
